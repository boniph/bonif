Experience

 
 
 
 
 

Post doc researcher
Diversity of abyssal polychaete assemblages from the nodule area of the North-eastern Pacific: spatial patterns, mechanisms of coexistence and preservation strategies

Ifremer

Sep 2017 – Mar 2019 Brest, France

Tasks:

  • Identification, DNA extraction and amplification of polychaetes sampled by box-corer
  • Ecological analysis on R (diversity estimators, constrained ordination, species range, betadiversity)


Aims of the first publication (1) to test the hypotheses that support spatial conservation planning in the CCFZ, particularly the environmental drivers of alpha and beta diversity such as organic carbon fluxes to the seafloor and nodule density; (2) to assess the representativeness of an APEI (i.e., APEI no. 3); and (3) to improve the assessment of potential risks of biodiversity loss due to nodule mining.

 
 
 
 
 

Post doc researcher
Diversity, distribution, phylogeny and connectivity of deep-sea polynoids from the nodule area of the North-eastern Pacific

Ifremer

Mar 2015 – Aug 2016 Brest, France

Tasks:

  • Benthic macrofauna sampling with ROV, epibenthic sledge and box-corer onboard of RV SONNE
  • Identification of polynoids and species descriptions
  • DNA extraction and amplification
  • Phylogenetic analysis on MrBayes, RAxML and PAUP


Aims of first publication: (1) to describe 17 new species of deep-sea polynoids using morphology together with molecular data in most cases (COI, 16S and 18S); and (2) to assess the monophyly of the subfamily Macellicephalinae.
